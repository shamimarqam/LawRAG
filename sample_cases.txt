
1. Boundary Dispute
	•	“Two adjacent landowners disagree about the exact boundary between their plots after a new fence was installed. The survey numbers match, but the physical markers seem shifted. What legal steps should be taken to resolve this?”
	•	“My neighbor claims 5 feet of my land belongs to him based on an old map. How do I verify the correct boundary and challenge the claim?”

2. Encroachment
	•	“A nearby shopkeeper has extended his structure onto my agricultural land. How can I prove encroachment and file a complaint?”
	•	“Someone constructed a temporary shed partially on my property while I was away. What actions can I take to remove the encroachment?”

3. Inheritance / Succession
	•	“After my father passed away, my siblings and I are disputing ownership of his farmland. No will exists. How is the property legally divided?”
	•	“One legal heir refuses to sign the mutation documents for inherited land. What remedies are available?”

4. Title / Ownership Dispute
	•	“Two different people claim ownership of the same plot due to conflicting sale deeds. How is the rightful owner determined?”
	•	“My land records show my name, but someone else has produced an older document claiming rights. What is the legal procedure to settle this?”

5. Government Acquisition Issues
	•	“The government issued a notice to acquire my land for a highway project, but the compensation seems unfair. How can I challenge the valuation?”
	•	“My land was marked as government land during a recent survey even though I have been farming it for years. How do I contest this?”

6. Tenancy / Lease Disputes
	•	“My tenant refuses to vacate the land even after the lease period expired. What is the legal process for eviction?”
	•	“The landlord is demanding higher rent suddenly without updating the lease agreement. How can this be challenged?”

7. Partition Disputes
	•	“My relatives and I jointly own ancestral property, and I want partition, but they are refusing. What legal steps can I take?”
	•	“After partition, the physical division of land is being contested. How is final demarcation handled legally?”

8. Fraud / Illegal Sale
	•	“A broker sold my land using forged documents. What legal action can I take to nullify the sale and prosecute the fraud?”
	•	“Someone impersonated me and registered a sale deed. How can I reclaim my land?”

9. Mutation / Record Errors
	•	“The land mutation shows the wrong name after sale. How do I correct it?”
	•	“Revenue records mistakenly list part of my land under another person’s name. What is the correction process?”

10. Easement Rights
	•	“My neighbor blocked the pathway I’ve used for years to access my field. Do I have an easement claim?”
	•	“A dispute has arisen over water access from a shared canal. What rights do each of us have?”
